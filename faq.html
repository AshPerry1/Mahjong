Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our mahjong lessons, private events, and services from Mahj Jen and Mahj Hen

Lesson Questions

How long are the lessons?

Both 101 and 102 lessons typically run 2-3 hours, depending on group size and questions. We ensure everyone has time to play at least one full game.

What's the difference between 101 and 102?

101 covers the basics: tiles, rules, and scoring. 102 builds on that with advanced strategies, Siamese and Patio play, and perfecting your technique.

Can I book a private lesson?

Absolutely! We offer private lessons for individuals or small groups. Contact us to discuss your specific needs and schedule.

Event Questions

How many people can participate?

Lessons work best with 4-8 people. For private events, we can accommodate larger groups and will adjust the format accordingly.

Do you travel for lessons?

Yes! We love traveling for private events. We've taught at homes, corporate offices, hotels, and resorts across the country.

Logistics & Policies

What should I bring to a lesson?

Just bring yourself! We provide all mahjong tiles, tables, and materials. For private events, we can discuss additional setup needs.

What's your cancellation policy?

We require 48 hours notice for cancellations. We're happy to reschedule for weather or emergencies.

Equipment & TML

Do you provide mahjong tiles?

Yes, we provide all equipment for lessons. We're also TML ambassadors and can help you purchase your own beautiful set.

How can I get my own TML tiles?

As official TML ambassadors, we can help you purchase beautiful mahjong tiles. Use our referral code "LOOKOUTMOUNTAIN" for special access to their premium collections.

Still Have Questions?

We're here to help! If you didn't find the answer you're looking for, reach out to Mahj Jen and Mahj Hen directly.
